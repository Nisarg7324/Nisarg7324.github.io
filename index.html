About Me

Hi, I am Nisarg Dave.

I am a cybersecurity professional dedicated to proactive defense and continuous learning. Most recently, I served as a Cybersecurity Analyst Lead at Resilience, Inc. where I architected Graylog SIEM and reduced organizational attack surface through vulnerability remediation. Additionally, my experience as a Graduate Teaching Assistant laid the foundation of communicating complex technical risks to a diverse audience.

I am constantly dissecting new threats, a habit I developed during my Master's at Rochester Institute of Technology (RIT) and through online learning platforms like TryHackMe, HackTheBox, SecurityBlueTeam, picoCTF, etc.

On this site, I share technical writeups for the challenges I solve. It serves as my public laboratory where I document my thought process while solving challenges of network security, digital forensics, OSINT, etc.

While I am currently exploring new career opportunities, I remain deeply immersed in the security community, whether that's through refining my SOC lab or documenting new challenges on this site. I am eager to bring the same energy and commitment to a forward-thinking security team.
